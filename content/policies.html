Late and Missing Assignments

  • All assignments are due before class begins one week after they are handed out unless you have been granted an extension due to unforeseen circumstances.
  • A late submission is defined as any work, including quizzes, submitted after the deadline.
    • For a late submission, the student will receive a 50% scaling of their grade for the each occurrence.
